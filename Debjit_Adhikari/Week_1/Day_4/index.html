My favourite TV series:

Here are some of my favourites. Click any of these for more information

  • Game Of thrones
  • The Vampire Diaries
  • Breaking Bad
  • Money Heist
  • Vikings

  • Game Of Thrones:-

    Nine noble families wage war against each other in order to gain control over the mythical land of Westeros. Meanwhile, a force is rising after millenniums and threatens the existence of living men.

  • The Vampire Diaries:-

    On her first day at high school, Elena meets Stefan and immediately feels a connection with him. However, what she does not know is that Stefan and his brother, Damon, are in fact vampires.

  • Breaking Bad:-

    Walter White, a chemistry teacher, discovers that he has cancer and decides to get into the meth-making business to repay his medical debts. His priorities begin to change when he partners with Jesse.

  • Money Heist:-

    When the national mint and a touring school group are held hostage by robbers, police believe that the thieves have no way out. Little do they know that the thieves have a bigger plan in store.

  • Vikings:-

    As Ragnar Lodbrok, a Norse farmer, carries out triumphant raids into English territory with the help of his fellow warriors, he ends up holding sway over the Vikings and becoming a Scandinavian king.
